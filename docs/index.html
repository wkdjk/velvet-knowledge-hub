ⓘ Why do different sources show different import volumes? ▶

Why the chart and table show different figures

The numbers are not directly comparable, and neither is wrong — they come from two distinct MFDS databases covering different import channels.

The A3 chart uses the MFDS annual statistics yearbook (식품의약품통계연보, Table 6-3-8), which records deer velvet imported as a herbal medicine or pharmaceutical ingredient. Figures show total annual value in USD, 2004–2024.

The records table uses the MFDS food-import portal (수입식품 정보마루), which records deer velvet imported as a food ingredient. These are individual shipment notifications filed under Korea's food safety system, 2016–2026.

A third channel also exists — sliced velvet under a separate product code (HS 0507.90.1190) — which neither source covers. Total Korean deer velvet imports are therefore higher than either source shows alone.
